Why in-person matters

Sure, you can watch talks online, but you can't replicate the energy of bumping into someone at lunch who's working on exactly the problem you're trying to solve. Or the late-night coding session that starts because someone mentions an interesting project during coffee break.

We're optimizing for connection. Nothing beats the hallway track.

What to expect

Talks from the community

Two days of presentations covering everything from beginner-friendly introductions to advanced deep dives. Our speakers come from across Africa and beyond, sharing their experiences, projects, and insights.

Advanced workshops

Hands-on sessions where you can dig deeper into specific topics. These are designed for people who want to get their hands dirty with code and learn by doing.

Open spaces

Some of the best conversations happen in the unstructured time. Open spaces let you organize discussions about whatever topics interest you - whether that's a specific technology, career advice, or just connecting with people working in similar areas.

The hallway track

This is where the magic happens. Those conversations by the coffee machine, the impromptu debugging sessions, the "hey, you should meet this person" introductions. We've designed our schedule with plenty of breaks and informal time because we know this is often the most valuable part of the conference.
